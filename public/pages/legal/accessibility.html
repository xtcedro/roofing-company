Accessibility Statement

Our commitment to ensuring our website is accessible to everyone.

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

1. Our Commitment to Accessibility

At Heavenly Roofing, we are committed to providing a website that is accessible to everyone, regardless of their abilities or disabilities. Our goal is to ensure that all users have a seamless experience while browsing our website and accessing our services.

2. Accessibility Standards

This website has been designed to comply with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA. These guidelines help ensure that content is accessible to a wider range of people with disabilities, including those with blindness, low vision, hearing loss, and cognitive disabilities.

3. Features of Our Accessible Website

  • Text alternatives for non-text content.
  • Keyboard navigation for all interactive elements.
  • Contrast ratios that make text and visuals readable.
  • Responsive design that adapts to different screen sizes and assistive technologies.

4. Ongoing Improvements

We are continuously working to improve the accessibility of our website. Feedback from our users is vital in helping us achieve our goals.
